# Module 4: Assessment and Case Studies

## Overview
This final module integrates all previous learning through comprehensive assessments, real-world case studies, and practical skill demonstrations to ensure competency in cardiovascular emergency care.

## Learning Outcomes
After completing this module, you will be able to:
- Integrate knowledge from all previous modules
- Apply clinical reasoning to complex scenarios
- Demonstrate proficiency in emergency cardiac care
- Complete comprehensive course certification requirements

## Module Structure

### 4.1 Comprehensive Case Studies (2 hours)
- Multi-system cardiac emergencies
- Complex ECG interpretation scenarios
- Team-based decision making
- Resource allocation and prioritization

### 4.2 Practical Skills Assessment (2 hours)
- ECG interpretation under time pressure
- ACLS scenario performance
- Communication and leadership skills
- Quality improvement considerations

### 4.3 Final Certification Exam (1 hour)
- Comprehensive written examination
- Practical skills demonstration
- Case presentation requirements
- Remediation opportunities if needed

## Assessment Components

### Written Examination (40%)
- 100 multiple choice questions
- ECG interpretation section
- Case-based scenarios
- Evidence-based practice questions

### Practical Skills (40%)
- ACLS mega-code scenarios
- ECG rhythm recognition
- Team leadership demonstration
- Communication effectiveness

### Case Study Presentation (20%)
- Complex patient scenario analysis
- Evidence-based treatment plan
- Risk-benefit assessment
- Quality metrics consideration

## Case Study Examples
- Acute STEMI with complications
- Cardiac arrest with ROSC
- Atrial fibrillation with hemodynamic instability
- Cardiogenic shock management
