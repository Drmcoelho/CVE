# CVE Course Learning Objectives

## Overall Course Objectives

By the end of this course, students will be able to:

1. **Recognize and assess cardiovascular emergencies** in clinical and pre-hospital settings
2. **Interpret ECG readings** accurately and identify life-threatening arrhythmias
3. **Apply evidence-based treatment protocols** for cardiac emergencies
4. **Demonstrate competency** in emergency cardiovascular care procedures

## Module-Specific Objectives

### Module 1: Introduction to Cardiovascular Emergencies
- Understand the anatomy and physiology of the cardiovascular system
- Identify risk factors for cardiovascular disease
- Recognize signs and symptoms of cardiac emergencies
- Understand the chain of survival concept

### Module 2: ECG Fundamentals and Interpretation
- Explain the electrical conduction system of the heart
- Identify normal ECG waveforms and intervals
- Recognize common arrhythmias and conduction abnormalities
- Interpret 12-lead ECG findings in emergency situations

### Module 3: Cardiac Emergency Management
- Apply ACLS (Advanced Cardiac Life Support) protocols
- Manage acute coronary syndromes (ACS)
- Treat cardiac arrest and arrhythmias
- Understand pharmacological interventions for cardiac emergencies

### Module 4: Assessment and Case Studies
- Integrate knowledge through practical case studies
- Demonstrate decision-making skills in emergency scenarios
- Apply learned concepts to real-world situations
- Complete comprehensive assessment of course material
